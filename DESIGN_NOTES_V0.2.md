# Fortune Shrine Design Direction Notes V0.2

## Current Assessment

The mythology exists. The blessings exist. The ritual exists. The payment flow exists. The philosophy exists.

But the sacred feeling has not fully arrived.

## Core Observation

The experience should feel less like:

- a temple destination
- a pantheon catalog
- a collection of gods

And more like:

- a threshold
- a sacred space
- a place between intention and action

## Threshold Principle

The Shrine exists in the space between intention and action.

Not after the outcome.

Not before the desire.

But in the final quiet moment before a choice is made.

## Guardian System Direction

Users do not collect gods. Users collect blessings.

The user should not need to choose the god.

The user enters.

The offering is made.

The ritual begins.

One guardian answers.

One blessing appears.

One oracle speaks.

Mystery increases. Meaning deepens.

## Sacred Space Research Direction

Do not focus on adding more gods.

Do not focus on adding more features.

Do not focus on adding more systems.

Research:

- sacred sound
- ritual timing
- light and shadow
- silence
- spatial layering
- atmosphere

Sacredness is rarely created by images alone. It is created by presence.

## Oracle Principle

The oracle does not explain itself.

The user completes the meaning.

This is not a flaw. This is the design.

## Final Reminder

Do not build a bigger temple.

Build a deeper feeling.
