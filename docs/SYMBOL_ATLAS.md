# Fortune Shrine Symbol Atlas

Version 1.0

Symbols should remain universal, ancient-feeling, and emotionally legible. They should not lock the experience to a specific religion, nation, profession, or market context.

## Flame

Meaning: warmth, presence, continuity, blessing kept alive by travelers.

Use for the sacred center, offering continuity, and hope in darkness.

Avoid implying guaranteed success or destructive force.

## Star

Meaning: distance, guidance without instruction, mystery, journey.

Use for cosmic scale and direction without advice.

Avoid implying fixed destiny or certainty.

## Moon

Meaning: reflection, incompleteness, cycles, quiet witness.

Use for soft, reflective, indirect oracle language.

Avoid when direct warmth or grounding is needed.

## Wind

Meaning: unseen movement, change, passage, subtle signs.

Use for transition and living uncertainty.

Avoid implying that the user should act.

## River

Meaning: flow, time, crossing, surrender without passivity.

Use before transition.

Avoid implying loss of agency.

## Mountain

Meaning: endurance, distance, humility, long path.

Use for patience, strength, and scale.

Avoid making the blessing too heavy.

## Dawn

Meaning: renewal, beginning, gentle hope.

Use for light without certainty.

Avoid implying everything will soon become good.

## Night

Meaning: uncertainty, solitude, mystery, emotional depth.

Use to acknowledge fear without judgment.

Avoid despair or horror.

## Silence

Meaning: sacred restraint, space for interpretation, divine non-interference.

Use at the end of oracle lines and in the ritual atmosphere.

Avoid when the experience already feels cold or empty.

## Door / Threshold

Meaning: before action, boundary between states, unknown ahead.

Use as the core ritual frame.

Avoid presenting the door as a gate to success, heaven, wealth, or certainty.

## Light

Meaning: recognition, warmth, witness.

Use to communicate "I see you."

Avoid suggesting full clarity or guaranteed answers.

## Shadow

Meaning: hidden truth, uncertainty, depth, humility.

Use to balance overly bright blessings.

Avoid punitive or ominous tone.

## Living Sky

Meaning: oracle diversity, many truths, light and shadow together.

Use for blessing library design.

Avoid exposing this complexity to the front-end user.
