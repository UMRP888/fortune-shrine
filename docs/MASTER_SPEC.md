# Fortune Shrine Master Specification

Version 1.1

## Product Definition

Fortune Shrine is a Digital Blessing Shrine.

It exists for those who are about to step into the unknown.

It offers neither permission nor prediction.

Only a blessing before the journey begins.

## Supreme Principle

> Blessing Is The Product. Everything Else Is Delivery.

Oracle, ritual, altar, gods, offering, archive, and visual system all exist to deliver blessing.

If any feature weakens the blessing, the feature should change.

Highest world rule:

```text
Presence Over Character
```

The Shrine is not a character, priest, oracle, avatar, guru, AI guide, or visible speaker.

The Shrine itself responds.

The Flame is the visible manifestation of the Shrine.

## User

The user is The One Standing Before Uncertainty.

The user may look like a trader, gambler, founder, traveler, student, or stranger, but the product should not rely on identity categories.

Design for emotion: waiting, hope, fear, loneliness, courage, uncertainty, and longing.

## Core Experience

The user should experience:

```text
Enter
Touch
Be seen
Receive blessing
Continue forward
```

The experience should take 5-15 seconds.

## MVP

Version 1 only needs:

```text
Landing
Sacred space
One action
Blessing
Optional offering
Exit
```

Everything else is later.

Postpone profiles, membership, token systems, rankings, community features, archives, complex payment tiers, and multiple ritual choices.

## Ritual

The ritual must have one core action.

Candidate actions:

- Touch The Flame
- Touch The Star
- Light The Flame

No tutorials. No complicated onboarding. No decision trees.

The user should not choose the god.

No guardian, priest, oracle, guide, or avatar should appear after the ritual.

The Shrine responds through flame, stillness, atmosphere, and blessing.

## Blessing Engine

Every blessing should follow:

```text
Witness
Blessing
Echo
```

Quality standard:

- Witness
- Blessing
- Echo
- Emotional truth
- Sacred tone
- Universal meaning
- No prediction
- No instruction
- No guarantee
- No judgment

Reject any line that sounds like trading advice, coaching, therapy, fortune-telling, motivational quotes, financial promises, or guaranteed outcomes.

## Voice

Tone balance:

```text
70% divine
30% human
```

The voice should feel ancient, compassionate, restrained, and emotionally present.

It should not feel cold, abstract, preachy, motivational, therapeutic, financial, or academic.

Target witness style:

- 70% silent witness
- 30% explicit witness

Reduce visible narrator presence.

## Oracle Diversity

The oracle should not feel repetitive.

Emotional mix:

- 40% warm blessings
- 20% wisdom blessings
- 15% cosmic blessings
- 15% balance blessings
- 10% melancholic blessings

The oracle should feel like a living sky: many different lights, shadows, silences, and truths.

## Visual Direction

The shrine should feel sacred, calm, solemn, mysterious, timeless, and universal.

Avoid tarot aesthetics, fortune teller aesthetics, purple crystal aesthetics, cheap mysticism, heavy religious specificity, gaming fantasy aesthetics, and financial dashboard aesthetics.

The shrine must feel warm.

The shrine must never feel exciting.

Avoid explosions, reward animations, jackpot sensations, gacha-style reveals, and particle storms.

MVP sound recommendation:

```text
No music.
```

Only extremely subtle ambience may be considered later.

Concept library:

1. The Gate Of The Unknown
2. The Eternal Flame
3. The Lone Star

Do not choose final visual direction yet. Preserve candidates.

## Offering System

Offering is not a purchase of certainty.

Offering is participation in the ritual.

Users are not paying for words.

Users are paying to light the flame.

The blessing follows the flame.

The sacred cycle:

```text
Traveler lights the flame
The Eternal Flame continues burning
Blessing follows the flame
Another traveler may one day receive light
```

Never frame offering as unlocking destiny, buying luck, increasing odds, securing success, or paying for prediction.

Frame offering as participation, support, contribution, and keeping the flame alive.

## Business Rules

Allowed revenue:

- richer ritual presentation
- more beautiful shrine spaces
- personal blessing archive
- annual blessing book
- ceremonial collectibles
- special seasonal rituals

Forbidden revenue:

- paid predictions
- VIP accuracy
- insider fortune
- wealth guarantee
- fear-based unlocks
- greed-based upsells
- streak punishment
- dependency loops

## Simplicity

Complexity behind.

Simplicity in front.

If a feature requires explanation, consider deleting it.

If a ritual requires teaching, simplify it.

If an experience requires thinking, make it more intuitive.

## Success Condition

The first shrine succeeds if a real user leaves feeling:

```text
I have been seen.
I have been acknowledged.
I have been blessed.
I am ready.
```

The user should not leave believing:

```text
I have been promised success.
I received financial advice.
I know the future.
```

## Current Mission

Build the first shrine.

Validate with real users.

Measure reactions to the first blessing.

Do not continue endless philosophy before the first shrine exists.
