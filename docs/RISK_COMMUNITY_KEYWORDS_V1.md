# Risk Community Keyword Pool v1

Version: Beta 0.1

Purpose: discover public posts from real people standing in risk, waiting,
uncertainty, loss, fear, or loss of restraint.

This pool does not look for betting opportunities, market direction, trade
signals, or people to encourage into further risk.

Constitution:

```text
Bless the state.
Never bless the outcome.
```

## Review Rules

Exclude promotions, referral links, signal groups, giveaways, news reposts,
automated accounts, generic market commentary, and posts celebrating or
encouraging harmful risk.

Human reviewers must judge the person and state behind the words. A keyword
match is only a discovery lead, never permission to contact someone.
